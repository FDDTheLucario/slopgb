# First class-A double-speed lift of the sub-dot event-phase ladder. The
# CGB double-speed FF41 mode bits read at the cc+2 MID phase: a third PPU
# edge `Ppu::m0_stat_flip` (set in m0_flip_events on SPRITE-extended lines,
